Essentials of Data Science



The Data Science Club, VIT Bhopal, organized a successful and informative hands-on workshop on “Essentials of Data Science” on 21st December 2021 virtually, where our resource person Dr. Sudha Subramani, gave a keynote lecture on Data Preprocessing, Data Exploration, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning techniques.
